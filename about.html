Ummah Care Foundation

Serving Humaity 

Ummah Care Foundation was the creation of Mr Feraz Younas as a vehicle to serve those less fortunate initially with the
assistance of friends and family. The organization was conceptualized in November 2013 and the first supporters were on
boarded in January 2014. The Organization is now registered with the Charities Commission (Number 1160905). Over
the last three years the Organisation has completed many projects both in the UK and overseas. 

Some highlights:
July 2014
Gaza conflict and suffering spurs Trustees in funf raising for emergency aid. Two convoys of emergency medical aid purchased and delivered into Gaza in cooperation with UNHCR partner, Arab Medical Union. In October a meat distribution project implemented in Gaza 

Nov 2014
UCF provides relief to flood victims in Pindi Bhattian, located in the Hafizabad district in the state of Punjab, Pakistan.  

Nov 2014 
UCF assists The Nest, an organisation that provides for unfortunate children that have no parents or just mothers who are not able to look after them 

Mar 2015
UCF exhibit at 12th Dubai International Humanitarian Aid & Development Conference & Exhibition. The event provides a forum for people of the humanitarian sector to interact, foster partnership, seek donors, share knowledge and gain information on the latest trends. 

Mar 2015
In recognition of the humanitarian effort of UCF, the United Kingdom Charities Commission has officially registered UCF under registration number 116090 

April 2015
UCF orphanage opened in Gunchathar Bala, Muzzafrabad with 35 children 

Sept 15
Meat distribution project to Rohingya Burmese Refugees in Bangladesh 

Sept 15 
UCF has followed the refugee trail from Calais to Greece to Lebanon, providing much needed aid ,including, 
winter packs and clothing (in collaboration with a local Primary School), meals in Austria,Croatia, Greece 
and Lebanon and sturdy shoes and baby food in Slovenia 
Dec 15 UCF volunteers spent two weekends in Glenridding, Pooley Bridge and Eamount Bridge in Cumbria 
alongside The Fire Fighters Charity with the huge clean up activity.

2016  
UCF has been providing water pumps to rural areas of Pakistan where small villages have no easy access 
to safe drinking water. 

Feb 2017
In conjunction with Dominos Pizza, UCF is providing a regular treat to the residents of St Basils, which is 
the largest charity focussing on vulnerable youths providing them support and accommodation..
